The Binging Channel

Turn on the hobby

Like many people do I get extremely into watching TV Shows up to the point of I start watching a few episodes and if I enjoy them I become addicted.


This usually entails me spending hours watching the full series or multiple seasons within a day or two.

Cut to Mid Season

Due to the reasons specified in the paragraph's above this hobby is most likely involved in other hobbies as I will then continue to watch a full series or multiple seasons on my phone or laptop.


This has allowed me to catch up on a lot of shows I get either recommended, introduced to or discovered. However, this has also caused me to rewatch many series a multitude of times, over and over again.

The Finale

So to summarise this hobby up..... it is never going to end, there wont be a finale, I will always continue to binge unless it's a show my partner wishes to watch.
