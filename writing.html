The Writing Corner

Lets Look at the Cover

Every good story has the beginning that starts the journey.


Mine started with writing when I was 11, this was when my spark for story telling, creative writing would be the blossoming of my hobby today.

Fantasizing Fiction

The first piece of fiction I wrote for myself as a child was created due to a random spark of creativity after finding a broken half of a key, this pushed me to enjoy writing later on in life as this story allowed me to open my mind to how words can impact a page, opening worlds and connecting a plethora of universes.


Even though I write less than I would like to now I do find myself creating love poems for my partner nearly every day as I find it an amazing way to be connected to her through a physical and unique collection of words that will only forever be for her eyes.

The Ending is Never

For the climax of my creative writing will never cease as I am forever igniting my passions in my life around me no matter the hardship, heart aches, weight of the world upon my shoulder.


I will continuosly write my poetry, think of book idea's and I will always do everything to stay creative even through a creative block and till the end of my days.
